<p><p>The small hydro turbine market includes several key types: Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are suitable for medium head applications and offer high efficiency. Kaplan turbines excel in low head situations with variable flow, utilizing adjustable blades for optimal performance. Pelton turbines are ideal for high head, low flow sites, utilizing a jet of water to drive the turbine. Other types may include cross-flow or Archimedes screw turbines, catering to specific site conditions and operational needs in small-scale hydroelectric projects.</p></p>
