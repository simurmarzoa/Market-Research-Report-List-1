<p><strong>The EPVC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymeric EPVC</li><li>Co-polymeric EPVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPVC resin market is primarily segmented into two types: Homopolymeric EPVC and Co-polymeric EPVC. Homopolymeric EPVC is characterized by its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, offering good mechanical and thermal properties, making it suitable for various applications like pipes and fittings. In contrast, Co-polymeric EPVC combines two or more vinyl monomers, enhancing flexibility, impact resistance, and chemical stability. This variant is often used in products requiring improved performance, such as automotive parts and high-quality film applications.</p></p>
