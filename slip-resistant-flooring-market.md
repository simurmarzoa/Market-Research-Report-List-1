<p><p>Slip-resistant flooring is categorized based on the coefficient of friction (CoF) ratings, which determine safety levels in various environments. R6 to R8 ratings indicate low to moderate slip resistance, suitable for residential areas. R9 rating offers increased grip, ideal for light commercial spaces. R10 rating provides moderate slip resistance for commercial settings, while R11 rating offers high resistance, suitable for wet or high-traffic areas. Each rating helps select appropriate flooring for specific applications, enhancing safety and reducing slip hazards.</p></p>
