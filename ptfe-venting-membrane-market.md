<p><p>The PTFE venting membrane market is primarily segmented into two types: adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents are designed with a sticky backing for easy application to various surfaces, ensuring an efficient seal while allowing gas or vapor release. On the other hand, vents without backing material require separate adhesives or methods for application, offering flexibility in usage but typically requiring more installation effort. Both types serve crucial roles in preventing pressure buildup in sealed environments.</p></p>
