<p><strong>The Oilfield Degasser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Oilfield Degasser</li><li>Portable Oilfield Degasser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield degasser market comprises two primary types: conventional and portable degassers. Conventional oilfield degassers are typically installed permanently at drilling sites, designed to efficiently remove gas from drilling fluids in large volumes, ensuring optimal operation. In contrast, portable oilfield degassers are mobile units that can be easily transported between sites, providing flexibility for temporary or smaller operations. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining safety and efficiency during oil and gas extraction by preventing gas accumulation in drilling fluids.</p></p>
