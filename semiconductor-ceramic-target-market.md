<p><ul><li>Planar Target</li><li>Rotary Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor ceramic target market is divided into planar and rotary target types, each serving distinct applications in thin-film deposition processes. Planar targets are flat surfaces that facilitate uniform coating across substrates, commonly used in microelectronics. In contrast, rotary targets feature a cylindrical shape, enabling continuous material supply during deposition, which enhances efficiency and productivity. Both types are crucial for producing high-quality semiconductor films, but their choice depends on specific manufacturing requirements and the scale of production desired.</p></p>
