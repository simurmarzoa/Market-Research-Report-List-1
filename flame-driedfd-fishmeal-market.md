<p><p>The Flame Dried (FD) fishmeal market consists of three main types: Whole Fish Meal, Semi-skimmed Fish Meal, and Defatted Fish Meal. Whole Fish Meal incorporates entire fish, retaining all nutrients and oils, making it rich in protein. Semi-skimmed Fish Meal has some oil and fat removed while maintaining a substantial nutrient profile. Defatted Fish Meal undergoes more extensive processing to eliminate fats, resulting in a higher protein concentration, suitable for applications requiring minimal lipid content. Each type serves specific nutritional needs in animal feed and aquaculture.</p></p>
