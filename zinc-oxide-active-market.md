<p><p>The zinc oxide active market encompasses three main production methods: the Direct Process (American Method), Indirect Process (French Method), and Wet Chemical Process. The Direct Process involves oxidizing zinc metal directly, resulting in high purity and low cost. The Indirect Process produces zinc oxide through vaporization and condensation, offering fine particle characteristics. The Wet Chemical Process utilizes chemical reactions in solution, allowing for controlled morphology and high surface area, catering to diverse industrial applications like rubber, ceramics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
