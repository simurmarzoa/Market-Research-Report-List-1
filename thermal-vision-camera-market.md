<p><strong>The Thermal Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal vision camera market includes three primary types based on wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras. Short-wave length cameras (SWIR) are effective in low-light conditions and can penetrate fog and smoke. Mid-wave length cameras (MWIR) are widely used in defense and surveillance, offering superior image quality. Long-wave length cameras (LWIR) are ideal for detecting heat signatures, commonly used in industrial applications and building inspections. Each type caters to specific needs based on environmental and operational requirements.</p></p>
