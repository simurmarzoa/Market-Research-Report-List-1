<p><p>The impregnate pitch market is categorized into coal tar pitch and petroleum pitch types. Coal tar pitch is derived from the carbonization of coal and is known for its high carbon content, making it suitable for applications in aluminum, graphite, and refractory industries. Petroleum pitch, obtained from the distillation of crude oil, is characterized by its lower carbon content and is primarily used in carbon electrodes, sealants, and waterproofing materials. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
