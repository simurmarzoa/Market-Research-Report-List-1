<p><p>The **Flat Glass Market** includes various types like **Ordinary Flat Glass**, **Float Glass**, and **Rolled Glass**. **Ordinary Flat Glass** is basic, often used in general applications. **Float Glass**, made by floating molten glass on molten tin, offers superior clarity and smoothness, ideal for windows and mirrors. **Rolled Glass** is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating textured or patterned surfaces, commonly used in decorative or privacy applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on quality, finish, and functionality.</p></p>
