<p><p>The Type A Molecular Sieve market includes 3A, 4A, and 5A molecular sieves, each classified by their pore sizes and adsorption properties. 3A molecular sieve, with a pore size of 3 angstroms, is ideal for adsorbing small molecules like water. The 4A molecular sieve, with a 4 angstrom pore size, effectively separates a broad range of gases and liquids.
