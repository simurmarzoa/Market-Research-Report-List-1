<p><p>The semiconductor wafer cleaning equipment market includes various types, notably the rotary wafer etching system and the manual wet batch system. The rotary wafer etching system utilizes a spinning mechanism to ensure uniform chemical application and efficient removal of contaminants, enhancing cleaning precision. In contrast, the manual wet batch system relies on manual processes for cleaning multiple wafers simultaneously in chemical baths, offering flexibility and lower setup costs. Both systems play crucial roles in achieving high-purity wafers essential for semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
