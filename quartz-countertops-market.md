<p><strong>The Quartz Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz countertops are primarily categorized into two manufacturing types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compacting a mixture of quartz crystals and resins under high pressure, resulting in a dense and durable surface. This method is efficient for mass production. In contrast, casting molding involves pouring the quartz mixture into molds and allowing it to set, offering greater design flexibility and unique appearances. Both types ensure durable, aesthetically appealing surfaces suitable for various applications in kitchens and bathrooms.</p></p>
