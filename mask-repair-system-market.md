<p><p>The Mask Repair System market comprises three main technologies: Laser Technology, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology, and Nanomachining Technology. Laser Technology employs beams of light for precise damage removal and repair on photomasks. FIB Technology utilizes focused ions to ablate or modify specific areas on a mask, offering high precision. Nanomachining Technology involves advanced techniques to create or repair features at the nanoscale, ensuring high-resolution and precision repairs. Together, these technologies enhance the performance and longevity of photomasks in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
