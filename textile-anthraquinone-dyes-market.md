Soluble anthraquinone dyes dissolve in water, making them suitable for various dyeing processes and applications, particularly in synthetic fibers. In contrast, insoluble anthraquinone dyes do not dissolve easily and are often used in specific applications where fixed color intensity is required, such as in plastics and coatings.
