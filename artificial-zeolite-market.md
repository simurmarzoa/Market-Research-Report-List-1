<p><p>Artificial zeolite is a synthetic, crystalline aluminosilicate material with a porous structure, widely used in applications such as catalysis, adsorption, ion exchange, and gas separation. Unlike natural zeolites, artificial zeolites are engineered to meet specific industrial requirements, offering superior performance and consistency.</p><p>The Artificial Zeolite Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
