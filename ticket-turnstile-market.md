<p><ul><li>Optical Turnstile</li><li>Arm Turnstile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ticket turnstile market is divided into two main types: optical turnstiles and arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles use advanced sensor technology to detect users, allowing access based on the authorized passage of individuals, making them ideal for high-traffic areas due to their seamless design and quick operation. In contrast, arm turnstiles feature rotating arms that physically restrict entry until a valid ticket is presented, providing a more controlled access point suitable for secure environments. Both types enhance security and manage crowd flow effectively.</p></p>
