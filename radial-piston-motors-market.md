<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Radial Piston Motors</li><li>Hydraulic Radial Piston Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial piston motors are specialized rotary devices that convert fluid pressure into mechanical power. They come in two primary types: pneumatic and hydraulic. Pneumatic radial piston motors utilize compressed air for operation, making them suitable for applications requiring lightweight and efficient performance, often in industries like automation. Hydraulic radial piston motors, on the other hand, operate using hydraulic fluid, providing high torque and power for heavy-duty tasks, commonly found in construction and manufacturing environments. Both types serve distinct operational needs.</p></p>
